About Set kenya.

Set Kenya was in 2017 registered as a club and Community Based organization. With a mandate to reach out to students across the country, empowering and mentoring them on an array of contemporary key aspects that will not only enlighten but also transform the life of student youth in our society into invaluable professionals.

Having previously existed as WESET (Western Student Empowerment Team) for seven years - from 2010 over to 2017, the organization carried out empowerment mission to schools in Western Kenya. This can attributed to vision and mission of the founders.

Over the years, the compulsion to reach out to more students and expanding our scope is what led to the conception of the Student Empowerment Team - Kenya.


Motto

Setting the Pace.

Vision

To empower the student youth into realizing their full potential as members of the society.

MIssion

To holistically take part in national development by sensitizing and motivating students into pursuing their due puporse through informed career choices.

Scope

1. Career Choices - There is a dire need for high school students to be properly informed of the different careers being offered in the various institutions of higher learning.

2. Social life - Students need to be encouraged to pursue education since knowledge is power. They shold not give up as ignorance is vey costly.

3. Christian living - Following the high rate of moral decandance in the youthful generation, there is a necessity for students to be duly exhorted on moral uprightness based on the Christian Biblical principles of which we subscribe to.

4. Current affairs and goal setting - The young generation ought to be made aware that the realization of Vision 2030 and in some way the Big Four Agenda lies squarely on their backs, hence they need to be empowered to position themselves for the same.

5. Mentorship - The students are to get a point of reference from those who are where where they are headed and have have been where they are now.

6. Discrete Issues - Any other distinct matters that might be facing the subject audience as specified by the various authorities and school adminstrations.





Mode of Operation

Conducting career and motivational talks to high school students and senior primary school pupils.

Engaging students in interactive open forums.

Sharing personal experiences and testimonies.

Question and Answer sessions.





Partner Organizations and Societies.

Western Outreach

Having been found by Western Outreach missioners, Set Kenya considers WESO to be its mother evangelistic team. Hence, all WESO evangelists subscribe to a constitutional 'free pass' as members of Set Kenya. Among other collaborations this two teams benefit from each other is fellowship, financial bail outs and team leadership.

The JKUAT Christian Union

Being a christian group and also a registered club, Set Kenya is by default a subset of the Christian as we depends upon them when sourcing for members within the Christian confine. The JKUAT Christian Union is somewhat the largest club with the largest subscription of christian student membership. Hence the dependance.

BEFEDO

Better Future Empowerment and Development Organization. As we both have a soft spot for student empowerment and mentorship, BEFEDO has become a bird of our feather ove the times. collaborations on missions, mentorships and other development activities is our modus operandi.
